Sarah Ibrahimi

PhD candidate

University of Amsterdam

I am a second-year PhD candidate in Computer Vision at the University of Amsterdam, supervised by Marcel Worring. My main area of interest is multimodal learning, visual reasoning and instance retrieval.

My background is in Computer Science with a specialization in Cyber Security (MSc 2014, Eindhoven University of Technology) and Mathematics (BSc 2012, Utrecht University). Besides, I have a background in Theater, Film and Television Studies (BSc 2012, Utrecht University).
